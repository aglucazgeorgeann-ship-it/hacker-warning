Step-by-Step Security Guide

1. Disconnect from Networks

Immediately turn off Wi-Fi and mobile data, or enable Airplane Mode. This prevents further unauthorized access to your device.

2. Backup Important Data

If possible, backup photos, documents, and contacts to a secure cloud service or external drive using another device.

3. Change Critical Passwords

Access your email and banking accounts from a different, trusted device. Change passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

4. Run Security Scans

Use your device's built-in security features (like Google Play Protect) or download reputable antivirus software from official app stores.

5. Contact Professionals

Call your mobile carrier, bank, or device manufacturer's support line. Report the incident and follow their guidance.
